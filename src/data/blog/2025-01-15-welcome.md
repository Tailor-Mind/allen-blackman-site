Welcome to my new website! This site replaces my previous Google Sites page and provides improved access to my research, publications, and web-based tools.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Research projects** organized by topic area, covering air and water pollution, land use and forest conservation, climate change, and land tenure
- **Publications** with links to PDFs, DOIs, and abstracts
- **Web tools** for forest conservation analysis, including the Forest Conservation Targeting Tool (FCTT) and Forest Conservation Evaluation Tool (FCET)
- **Professional links** to organizations and institutions in environmental economics

I will be updating this site regularly with new research findings and policy commentary. Stay tuned!
